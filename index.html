Bookstores

Kitchen Arts and Letters

Kitchen Arts & Letters is a New York City bookstore specializing in food and drink. Much more than cookbooks, the store covers a range of food history and scholarship, a selection of operations and technical manuals, and great food writing. They also track down out-of-print and scarce books upon request.
